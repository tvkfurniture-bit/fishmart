Our Story

Garia's Trusted Kitchen Partner

We started Garia Fish Fix with a single goal: let Bengalis enjoy their favorite daily meals without the drudgery and smell of fish prepping. We treat every order as if it's going to our own home kitchen. Hygienic, honest, and hyper-local to the core.

Frequently Asked Questions

We source daily at 4:00 AM from local wholesale hubs. It is processed immediately under temperature-controlled environments and delivered to you within hours.

Our marinated packs are best if cooked within 24 hours of delivery. If frozen, they can last up to 3 days, but we recommend cooking fresh!
